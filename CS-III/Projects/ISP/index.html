Our projects is an electric motorcycle constructed with a base from a 2002 Kawasaki Ninja. Responsibility will be divided between mechanical, electrical, and software aspect. Mechanical responsibilities are mostly consisting of disassembly and mounting motors/batteries. Electrical responsibilities will be wiring the ESC and motors as well as designing the battery packs and balance system. Software will flash the ESC and will be a firmware-swapped MCU, and will be responsible for the PID, speed and battery monitoring.
